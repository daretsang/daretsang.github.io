Darren Tsang

About

Darren Tsang graduated in 2024 from the University of British Columbia with a B.Sc. in Statistics and Environmental Science. While completing his degree, Darren worked as a GIS Technician co-op student for the Ministry of Forests, where he conducted watershed analysis for the hydrology team. He also worked as a GIS co-op student for the Vancouver Airport Authority, where he helped to maintain and update their web GIS. Darren is eager to apply his GIS and data analysis skills in the mining or other natural resources industries, but is also interested in working within a municipal government.
